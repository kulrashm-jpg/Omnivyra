## ✅ **THE TRIGGER: "Convert to Weekly View" Button**

### **🎯 What is the Trigger?**

The trigger is a **prominent orange button** that appears in your Campaign AI Assistant when AI provides content suggestions with a "Content Calendar Template".

### **📍 Where You'll See It:**

1. **In your Campaign AI Assistant** (the dialog you have open)
2. **Below AI messages** that contain content suggestions
3. **When AI provides** platform-specific content recommendations

### **🔍 How to Trigger It:**

1. **Ask AI for content suggestions** like:
   - "Create a 12-week content plan"
   - "Suggest content for LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube"
   - "Generate a content calendar template"

2. **Look for the AI response** that includes:
   - Platform-specific content types
   - Content Calendar Template
   - Weekly content suggestions

3. **Click the "Convert to Weekly View" button** that appears below the AI message

### **🎨 What the Button Looks Like:**

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│  📅 Convert to Weekly View         │
│  Generate 12-week content plan     │
│  from AI suggestions               │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### **⚡ What Happens When You Click:**

1. **Redirects** to hierarchical navigation page
2. **Shows success message**: "AI content suggestions have been converted to weekly view!"
3. **Opens 12-week overview** with AI-generated content
4. **Enables navigation** through: 12 Weeks → Week → Day views

### **🔄 Complete Flow:**

```
Campaign AI Assistant
    ↓ (Ask for content suggestions)
AI Response with Content Calendar
    ↓ (Click "Convert to Weekly View" button)
Hierarchical Navigation (12 Weeks → Week → Day)
    ↓ (Navigate and refine)
Daily Content Plans
```

### **✨ The Button Features:**

- **Orange gradient** design (stands out)
- **Calendar icon** (visual cue)
- **Hover effects** (scale and shadow)
- **Clear description** of what it does
- **Only appears** when AI provides content suggestions

**The trigger is now ready!** When you get AI content suggestions in your Campaign AI Assistant, you'll see the "Convert to Weekly View" button appear below the AI message. 🚀
